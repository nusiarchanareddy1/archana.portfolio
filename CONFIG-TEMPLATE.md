## 💡 Tips for Completion

1. **Be Honest** - Accurately represent your skills
2. **Be Specific** - Use concrete examples
3. **Show Impact** - Explain results and outcomes
4. **Keep Updated** - Add new projects regularly
5. **Be Professional** - Use clear, concise language
6. **Optimize Links** - Ensure all links are working
7. **Mobile First** - Check how it looks on phones
